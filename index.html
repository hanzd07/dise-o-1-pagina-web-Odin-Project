Landscapes

This page is dedicated to showing landscapes around the world.

Natural landscapes have the power to inspire and amaze people. A website dedicated to them can be a constant source of beauty and serenity.

Mountains are formed through geological processes such as tectonic uplift, volcanic activity, and erosion. The movements of tectonic plates are one of the main causes of mountain formation.

Sunflowers are native to North America and were domesticated by indigenous peoples thousands of years ago. Today, they are cultivated worldwide. A notable characteristic of young sunflowers is their ability to follow the movement of the sun throughout the day, a phenomenon known as heliotropism.

Snow-capped mountains are typically found at high altitudes where the temperature is low enough to maintain snow and ice year-round.

Waterfalls are formed when a river or stream flows over a layer of hard rock that is situated over a layer of soft rock. Over time, the soft rock erodes more quickly, creating a drop and, eventually, a waterfall.
